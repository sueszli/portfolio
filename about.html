about me
/001

Yo! I've always loved pita bread with hummus but my love for computer science is a quite recent one that I discovered 2017, after being introduced to the Maker-Community.

There I experienced the true joy of tinkering with tech for the first time and then (after some serious self-reflection) committed myself to learning everything I can learn about it on my journey.

I'm currently studying Bsc. Software and Information Engineering @ Vienna University of Technology.

interests
/002

     software engineering, machine learning, data science, generative art, data visualization and communication, typography, UI/UX, cognitive science, blockchain and finance, ultimate frisbee

strengths
/003

When looking at an artwork one can immediately tell whether the artist was comfortable and confident in what they were doing - or adding unnecessary features just to prove themselves.

For this very reason I value the feeling of comfort and ease in the making-process just as much as I value the discomfort of challenging myself.

Apart from redundancy I also dont like big words and convolute sentences and have a habit of summarizing everything I learn in its simplest form and explaining it to myself (or other colleagues if possible) and consider this ability to be my biggest strength.
