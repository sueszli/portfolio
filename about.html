Howdy, internet stranger!
I'm Yahya or Jan for short and I like to make stuff.

I currently reside in Vienna, Austria where I study Software Engineering @ the Vienna University of Technology.

There I experienced the true joy of tinkering with tech for the first time and then committed myself to the journey I am on today.
